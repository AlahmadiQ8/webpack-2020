Create & Share Your Personal Skincare Routine

A site to build your personal skincare routine that is sharable via an image, permalink, or print

create Create Routine

I am a full-time software engineer with a passion for skin-care. After being overwhelmed with researching skincare routines in the web, I built this site as a platform to easily store and share community-created routines that anyone can easily download and refer to later.

This is my own side project that I enjoyed putting together. It may not really solve a real problem. However, if you think of a feature to make it more useful let me know! Here are current and upcoming features this site provides:

  • check_box Permalink, image, and print sharing
